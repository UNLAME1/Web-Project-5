My Top 3 N64 Games!

Check out these awesome classics that I grew up playing!

1. Super Mario 64

Super Mario 64 was one of the first 3D games and changed gaming forever. I love the soundtrack and the goal is to play the game to collect stars!

Source: YouTube

2. The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time

This game is legendary for its great storyline and exciting gameplay. You play as Link and travel through time and save Hyrule!

Source: YouTube

3. GoldenEye 007

GoldenEye 007 was one of the earliest first-person shooters on consoles. You are James Bond in action and you take down enemies!
